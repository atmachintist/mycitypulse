# MyCityPulse Quick Start Guide

Welcome to MyCityPulse! This guide will help you get started in just a few minutes.

---

## 🚀 Getting Started (5 minutes)

### Step 1: Search Your City
1. Look at the top of the page - you'll see a **search bar**
2. **Type your city name** (e.g., "Delhi", "Mumbai", "Bangalore")
3. **Select from suggestions** that appear
4. Press **Enter** or **click on the city**

### Step 2: Explore Your City's Profile
Once you select a city, you'll see:
- 📊 **Key Statistics** - Population, density, area
- ⚠️ **Civic Stress Level** - Current challenges
- 🏙️ **City Type** - Urban typology and character
- 👥 **Civic Organizations** - Local NGOs and groups

### Step 3: Find Your Ward
1. Scroll to the **"Find Your Ward"** section
2. **Enter your address or area** (e.g., "Bandra", "Sector 12")
3. Select your ward from results
4. See your **local corporator's contact details**

### Step 4: Report an Issue (Optional)
If you see a public space problem:
1. Click **"Report Issues"** button
2. Choose the **issue type** (litter, garbage, pothole, etc.)
3. **Describe the problem** briefly
4. **Take a photo** (optional but helpful)
5. **Submit** - it goes to the right authorities

## 💡 Pro Tips

### Search Tips
- **Not sure of spelling?** Don't worry - search is flexible
  - "Blore" → finds "Bangalore"
  - "Bombay" → finds "Mumbai"
  - "New Delhi" → finds "Delhi"

- **Can't find your city?** We cover 62+ cities currently
  - Check the "All Cities" list
  - Let us know if you want your city added

### Reading City Pages
- **Too much information?** Scroll gradually
- **Want quick overview?** Read the one-liner and stress level first
- **Need details?** Click to expand sections
- **Found something useful?** Share with friends

### Ward Selection
- **Unsure of your ward?** Ask a local neighbor
- **Different names?** Some wards have multiple names (we handle that)
- **Can't find it?** Use the search - it's flexible
- **Still stuck?** Use area names (neighborhoods, local landmarks)

### Issue Reporting
- **Be specific** - Better descriptions help faster resolution
- **Add photos** - Visual proof speeds up action
- **Include location** - Be as precise as possible
- **One issue per report** - Makes tracking easier

### Comparing Cities
- **Choose 2-4 cities** - More than 4 gets hard to compare
- **Focus on one metric** - Compare similar types of cities
- **Use statistics wisely** - Numbers tell a story but aren't everything
- **Remember context** - Urban challenges differ by city type

## 🎯 Common Questions

### Q: How often is data updated?
A: City statistics update quarterly. Issue data updates as reported. Elections update closer to voting dates.

### Q: Is my report private?
A: Your report is submitted to municipal authorities. We don't publish personal information but may analyze trends anonymously.

### Q: Can I see my ward's election results?
A: Yes! For cities with election data, select the "Elections" tab to see results and candidate information.

### Q: How is "Civic Stress" calculated?
A: It combines:
- Population density
- Infrastructure gaps
- Environmental challenges
- Governance capacity
- Recent issues reported

### Q: Can I report issues in any city?
A: Currently, issue reporting works in select cities. Check if your city has this feature on its page.

### Q: How do I share information?
A: Use your browser's share button, or copy the page URL to share with friends on social media.

### Q: Is MyCityPulse affiliated with the government?
A: No. We're an independent platform that helps citizens understand their cities better.
